Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start charging at a ChargeNet EV station?

Simply download our mobile app, create an account, add a payment method, and scan the QR code at any station. You can also use our website to find and book stations in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. You can also pre-load funds to your ChargeNet EV wallet.

How much does it cost to charge?

Pricing varies by station type and location. Standard charging starts at $0.20/kWh, fast charging at $0.30/kWh, and ultra-fast DC charging at $0.40/kWh. Check individual station details for exact pricing.

Can I reserve a charging slot in advance?

Yes! Premium and Business plan members can reserve charging slots up to 24 hours in advance. Basic members can book same-day slots subject to availability.

What if my charging session is interrupted?

If your session is interrupted due to technical issues, you won't be charged for the incomplete session. Our 24/7 support team can help resolve any issues immediately.

Do you offer fleet charging solutions?

Yes, we offer comprehensive fleet charging solutions with dedicated account management, custom pricing, and advanced analytics. Contact our business team for a customized quote.
